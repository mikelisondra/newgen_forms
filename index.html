Terms and Conditions

These terms and conditions outline the rules and regulations for the use of New Gen Dental Clinic, which is located at Balgaan Place 1, Mayaman corner Kalayaan St., Quezon City, Philippines and 36C 2nd Floor L. De Guzman St. Concepcion Uno, Marikina City, Philippines.

  1. Scope of Services

    1.1 The dental clinic provides a range of dental services, including odontectomy, oral prophylaxis, dentures, braces, crowns/bridges/veneers.

  2. Appointments and Cancellations

    2.1 Patients are required to schedule appointments in advance.
    2.2 Cancellations or rescheduling must be made at least 24 hours before the appointment time.
    2.3 Failure to provide timely notice of cancellation may result in a cancellation fee.

  3. Payment Terms

    3.1 Payment is due at the time of service.
    3.2 Accepted payment methods include cash and credit/debit cards.

  4. Treatment Plans and Consent

    4.1 Treatment plans will be discussed with patients, outlining recommended procedures, associated costs, and expected outcomes.
    4.2 Patients are encouraged to ask questions and provide informed consent before undergoing any treatment.

  5. Privacy and Confidentiality

    5.1 The dental clinic respects patient privacy and complies with applicable privacy laws.
    5.2 Patient information is securely stored and only disclosed as necessary for treatment, billing, or with the patient's consent.

  6. Patient Responsibilities

    6.1 Patients must provide accurate and complete medical history information.
    6.2 Patients are responsible for following oral care instructions, attending scheduled appointments, and promptly notifying the clinic of any changes in health or medications.

  7. Complaints and Disputes

    7.1 Patients with concerns or complaints are encouraged to discuss them directly with the clinic's management.
    7.2 In the event of a dispute, the parties agree to resolve it amicably through negotiation or mediation.

  8. Liability Limitations

    8.1 The dental clinic shall not be held liable for any risks or complications that are not associated with dental procedures.
    8.2 Patients understand and accept that certain inherent risks exist in dental treatment.

  9. Termination of Services

    9.1 The dental clinic reserves the right to terminate its services to a patient in cases of non-compliance with treatment plans, failure to follow clinic policies, or disruptive behavior.

  10. Intellectual Property

    10.1 All trademarks, logos, and proprietary content associated with the dental clinic are protected.
    10.2 Unauthorized use, reproduction, or modification of clinic materials is strictly prohibited.
